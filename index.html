Who is boynextdoor?

BOYNEXTDOOR means 'the boys next door' and is KOZ's first boy group consisting of 6 members: Seongho, Liu, Myung Jaehyun, Taesan, Lee Han, and Unhak. Just like the familiar name, they seek empathy by talking about daily life through easy-to-listen yet original music, and expressing various emotions in it.

BOYNEXTDOOR believes that the essence of music lies in the exchange of emotions. When I turn on the music, I listen without even realizing it, nod my head to the lyrics that remind me of my work, awaken emotions I had forgotten about, and look back on my own time. This kind of 'empathy' is what BOYNEXTDOOR wants to reach through music.

So free and honest, BOYNEXTDOOR focuses on the stories of young people at this time.
